How to Upload Policies

This is your first and most important step. Follow this flow to get your customer policies onto the platform.

Find the Upload Button: If you're a new user, click the main "Get Started" button. If you're an existing user, find "Upload new policy" at the top right of the dashboard.

Use the Template: It is crucial to download the sample .XLS template first. Your policy data spreadsheet must be formatted with the exact same columns and structure to ensure a successful upload.

Select Your File: Click the upload prompt and select your prepared spreadsheet, or simply drag and drop the file into the designated area.

Confirmation: After a successful upload, the button will change to "View policies." Clicking it will take you directly to the Policies Tab where you can see all your data.

How to Create Inspection & Claim Links

Generate links for customers to complete pre/post-loss inspections or to initiate a claim. The process is identical for both.

One-Time Link

For a single, specific policy. It can only be used once.

Global Link

A general link that can be used for multiple policies and instances.

1. Go to Inspections/Claims Tab & Click 'Create Link'
‚Üì
2. Choose Link Type (One-Time or Global)
‚Üì
3. Select Policy (for One-Time) or Confirm (for Global)
‚Üì
4. Success! Link is Generated

Start the Process: From either the Inspections or Claims tab, click the "Create Link" button to open the creation modal.

Select Link Type: In the pop-up, choose whether you need a "One-Time Link" for a specific policy or a reusable "Global Link." Click "Proceed."

Finalize the Link: If you chose "One-Time," a list of your policies will appear; find the correct one and click "Create Link." If you chose "Global," simply review the details and click "Generate Link."

Confirmation: Your new link is now created and will appear in the list on the current tab and also in the main "Links" tab.

How to Manage Your Links

The Links Tab is your central hub for all generated links. From here, you can view details, disable, or delete them.

1. Go to Links Tab & Find Your Link
‚Üì
2. Click 'Details' Under the Actions Column
‚Üì
3. Choose Action in Sidebar (Disable or Delete)
‚Üì
4. Success! Link is Updated

Locate the Link: Navigate to the "Links" tab. Find the specific link you wish to manage in the master list.

View Details: In the row for that link, find the "Actions" column and click on the "Details" button.

Take Action: A "Link Information" sidebar will appear. From here, you can choose to "Disable Link" (making it inactive) or "Delete Link" (removing it permanently).

Confirmation: The link's status will be updated in the system immediately according to the action you took.
