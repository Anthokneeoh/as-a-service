How to View and Filter Policies

Once your policies are uploaded, the 'Policies' tab on the services menu page allows you to:

  • View a comprehensive list of all customer policies.
  • Filter between 'Active' and 'Expired' policies using the sub-tabs.
  • Use the search bar to find specific policies and export the visible data for your records.

How to Manage Inspections

The 'Inspections' tab on the services menu page is where you create and track links for pre-loss verification or post-loss assessment.

Key Action: Creating an Inspection Link

Step 1: Click "Create Link"

Navigate to the 'Inspections' tab within the 'Services' section of the MCA dashboard. Click the CTA 'Create Link' button.

Step 2: Choose Your Link Type

A modal will appear. Select either a One-Time Link (for a single policy, used once) or a Global Link (reusable for multiple policies).

Step 3: Generate the Link

For a One-Time Link, you will be shown a list of your policies; select the desired one to proceed. For a Global Link, you will confirm details on the next screen and then generate it.

Step 4: Success!

Your inspection link is now created and will appear in the list on both the 'Inspections' and 'Manage Links' tabs.

How to Manage Claims

The 'Claims' tab on the services menu page allows you to create claim links, monitor the status of all submissions, and process them through to resolution.

Key Action: Creating a Claim Link

Step 1: Click "Create Link"

On the 'Claims' tab, click the 'Create Link' button.

Step 2: Choose Your Link Type

Select either a 'One-Time Link' for a specific policy claim or a 'Global Link' for general claim submissions.

Step 3: Success!

Your claim link is now active and ready to be shared with your customer.

Key Action: Processing and Paying a Claim

Step 1: Navigate to the Claim

From the 'Claims' tab list inside the services menu page, find the claim you wish to process and click on 'Details' in the 'Actions' column.

Step 2: Review Claim Details

On the claim details page, you can review damaged parts, approve post-loss inspection images, and request additional information if needed.

Step 3: Manage the Claim

Use the 'Manage Claim' panel on the right side of the screen to carry out actions. Here you will find options to 'Approve', 'Reject', or 'Pay' the claim.

Step 4: Export & Review Logs

Above the management panel, you have options to 'Export Claim Doc' for your records or view the 'Claim Log' to see a full history of actions taken on that claim.

How to Manage Links

The 'Links' tab on the services menu page is your central hub for managing all inspection and claim links you have created. Here you can view their status, validity and usage.

Key Action: Managing an Existing Link

Step 1: Locate the Link

On the 'Links' tab, switch between Inspection and Claims to filter the list, then select the link you want to manage in the main table.

Step 2: Click "Details"

In the 'Actions' column for that link, click the 'Details' button.

Step 3: Choose Your Action

A 'Link Information' panel will appear. From here, you can Disable the link to deactivate it or Delete it permanently.

How to Manage Your Team

The 'Settings' section, found at the bottom of the left-hand menu on the MCA dashboard, is for administrative control over your organization's access to the platform.

Key Action: Onboarding a Team Member

Step 1: Navigate to Team Management

On the MCA dashboard, click on 'Settings' in the left-hand menu, then select the 'Team Management' tab.

Step 2: Invite Member

Click the 'Invite a member' button. Note: If you have no roles created yet, you must first create a role before inviting a member.

Step 3: Fill out the Invitation Form

Enter the new member's details, set a temporary password, and assign them an 'Access Level' by selecting a pre-defined role from the dropdown.

Step 4: Send Invite

Click the 'Invite Team Member' button to send the invitation. They will now appear in your list of team members.

Key Action: Creating Roles & Permissions

Step 1: Navigate to Roles

In 'Settings' > 'Team Management', click the 'Roles' button.

Step 2: Create a New Role

Click the 'Create New Role' button to open the creation modal.

Step 3: Define the Role and Permissions

Give the role a name (e.g., "Claims Adjuster"). Then, check the boxes for the specific permissions you want to grant for Claims and Inspections.

Step 4: Save the Role

Click 'Create Role' to save. This role can now be assigned to team members.
