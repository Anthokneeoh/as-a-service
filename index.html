Your Digital Filing Cabinet

The Policies tab is where all your uploaded policies are stored and organized. You can search for specific policies, filter them by status (like 'Active' or 'Expired'), and view detailed information for each entry. This section acts as your central repository, ensuring all policy data is accessible and manageable in one place.

Key Features:

  • Comprehensive List: View all customer policies with details like name, policy ID, and status.
  • Status Filtering: Quickly toggle between 'Active' and 'Expired' policies to manage your portfolio.
  • Search & Export: Use the search bar to find specific policies and export the visible data for your records.
  • Upload Policies: You can also initiate the policy upload process directly from this tab. The flow is identical to the one on the Dashboard.

Managing Pre & Post-Loss Inspections

The Inspections tab is your hub for creating and tracking inspection links. These links can be generated for either pre-loss verification (before a policy is active) or post-loss assessment (as part of a claim). The system provides flexibility by allowing you to create links for single, specific policies or global links for broader use.

Key Action: Creating an Inspection Link

Follow this interactive guide to understand how to generate either a One-Time or Global inspection link.

1. Click "Create Link"

Start by clicking the prominent "Create Link" button on the Inspections tab.

‚Üì

2. Choose Your Link Type

A modal will appear. Select either:

  • One-Time Link: For a specific, single policy. Can only be used once.
  • Global Link: A reusable link for multiple policies and instances.

3a. For One-Time Links

After clicking "Proceed", you'll see a list of your active policies. Find the correct one and click "Create Link" in its row.

3b. For Global Links

After clicking "Proceed", you'll confirm details for the multi-use link and then click "Generate Link".

4. Success!

Your inspection link is now created and will appear in the list on the Inspections tab and the Manage Links tab.

Tracking Every Claim

The Claims tab provides a comprehensive view of all customer claims submitted through the platform. Here, you can monitor the status of each claim, from initial submission to final resolution. Use the status filters ('Paid', 'In Progress', 'Rejected') to quickly organize the data and stay on top of your claims lifecycle.

Your Link Management Hub

The Links tab is your central dashboard for managing all the inspection and claim links you have created. From here, you can view the status of every link, see its usage, and perform administrative actions like disabling or deleting links that are no longer needed. This ensures you have full control over the access you provide.
